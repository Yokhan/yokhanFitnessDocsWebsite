# Domain: Business & Startups & Finance — Evidence-Based Guard

## NEVER Recommend (Myths & Anti-patterns)
1. **Scaling Before PMF** — 74% of failed startups scaled prematurely (Startup Genome, n=3,200). Instead: validate with 40%+ retention first.
2. **Ignoring Unit Economics** — If LTV < CAC, every customer accelerates death. Instead: know CAC, LTV, payback period from day one.
3. **Paying Only Credit Card Minimums** — $5K at 22% APR: most payments go to interest for years. Instead: 2-3x minimum, avalanche method.
4. **Timing the Market** — Missing just 10 best days in 20 years cuts returns by ~50% (Dalbar). Instead: stay invested, dollar-cost average.
5. **No Emergency Fund** — 56% of Americans can't cover $1,000 emergency. Instead: 3-6 months expenses in liquid savings before investing.
6. **Hiring Too Fast** — Early scalers more likely to fail with no benefit (Wharton/SMJ 2024). Instead: hire only at proven bottlenecks.
7. **Single Channel Dependency** — Platform changes kill businesses overnight (FB organic: 16%->2%). Instead: no channel >40% of revenue.
8. **Whole Life Insurance as Investment** — Fees eat 50-70% of first-year premium. Instead: term life + invest the difference in index funds.
9. **Active Fund Management** — 90% underperform S&P 500 over 15 years (SPIVA). Instead: low-cost index funds (Vanguard, Fidelity).
10. **Building Before Selling** — 43% of startup failures = no market need. Instead: validate demand with landing pages/pre-sales first.

## ALWAYS Apply (Evidence-Based Principles)
1. **Validate Before Building** — Landing pages, pre-sales, concierge MVPs. Catch "no market need" at near-zero cost.
2. **Pay Yourself First** — Automate 20%+ savings before spending. Behavioral nudge that works (Thaler & Benartzi).
3. **Retain > Acquire** — 5% retention improvement = 25-95% profit increase. Existing customers cost 5-25x less than new ones.
4. **Master One Distribution Channel** — Thiel: "If you can get even a single channel to work, you have a great business."
5. **Index Fund Core** — Bogle/Buffett: broad market index funds beat 90% of active managers. Keep expense ratios <0.2%.
6. **Price on Value, Not Cost** — Value-based pricing captures 15-25% more revenue. Test pricing quarterly.
7. **Disagree and Commit** — Debate vigorously, decide quickly, execute fully. Decision velocity > decision perfection.
8. **18+ Months Cash Runway** — Median time last-raise-to-death is 22 months (CB Insights). Cash = options.
9. **Fire Fast, Hire Slow** — One toxic employee reduces team performance 30-40% (Harvard). Structured interviews, 30/60/90 reviews.
10. **Diversify Across Asset Classes** — Markowitz: portfolio diversification is the only free lunch in finance. Stocks + bonds + real estate.
