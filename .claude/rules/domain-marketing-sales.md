# Domain: Marketing & Sales — Evidence-Based Guard

## NEVER Recommend (Anti-patterns)
1. **Vanity Metrics** — Impressions/followers have near-zero correlation with revenue. Instead: track CAC, LTV, ROAS, conversion rate.
2. **Content Without Distribution** — 4.6B pieces published daily; zero distribution = zero visibility. Instead: 50/50 rule (creation:distribution).
3. **SEO Gaming** — Google March 2024 update demoted 45% of low-value sites. Instead: genuinely helpful content, human-refined.
4. **Spray-and-Pray Cold Outreach** — Mass untargeted emails destroy domain reputation. Instead: personalized, researched, value-first outreach.
5. **Marketing Qualified Leads (MQL) Theater** — MQL-to-close rates often <1%. Instead: revenue-qualified signals, buying intent data.
6. **Discounting to Close** — Trains buyers to wait; erodes margins permanently; 2x higher churn. Instead: add value, don't cut price.
7. **Ignoring Existing Customers** — Acquisition costs 5-25x retention. Instead: invest in retention, expansion, and customer success.
8. **Single Channel Dependency** — Platform algorithm changes can kill overnight (FB organic: 16% to 2%). Instead: no channel >40% revenue.
9. **Feature-Dumping Sales Pitches** — Nobody cares about your features. Instead: SPIN Selling (Situation, Problem, Implication, Need-Payoff).
10. **Fake Urgency/Scarcity** — Countdown timers that reset destroy trust. FTC increasingly targets this. Instead: genuine limited offers only.
11. **Batch-and-Blast Email** — Sending same email to entire list. DETECT: open rates <15%, unsubscribe >0.5%/send. Instead: segment by behavior, personalize, test subject lines.
12. **B2B Content Written Like B2C** — Emotional impulse copy for 6-12 month enterprise sales cycles with 5+ stakeholders. Instead: educational content addressing each buyer persona's specific concerns.
13. **No Content Strategy, Just Content Calendar** — Publishing on schedule without strategic narrative or buyer journey mapping. Instead: map content to awareness/consideration/decision stages for each ICP.
14. **Overweighting New Logos vs Expansion** — Spending 80% of GTM budget on acquisition when expansion costs 1/5 to 1/7 of new. Instead: invest in upsell/cross-sell and customer success.
15. **Confusing Pipeline With Revenue** — Counting unqualified leads as near-revenue. B2B close rates are 15-30%; 70-85% of pipeline vanishes. Instead: weight by stage probability, track conversion rigorously.

## ALWAYS Apply (Principles)
1. **Mental Availability + Physical Availability** — Byron Sharp: brands grow by being easy to think of AND easy to buy.
2. **60/40 Brand/Performance Split** — Binet & Field (IPA): 60% brand building, 40% activation maximizes long-term growth.
3. **Broad Reach Over Tight Targeting** — Sharp: most growth comes from light/non-buyers, not hyper-targeted loyalists.
4. **SPIN Selling for Complex Sales** — Uncover Situation, Problem, Implication, Need-Payoff. Questions > pitches.
5. **Challenger Sale Model** — Teach customers something new about their business; reframe their thinking.
6. **Multi-Touch Attribution** — No single touchpoint drives conversion. Track full journey across channels.
7. **Content-Market Fit Before Scale** — Test content resonance with small audience before scaling distribution.
8. **Retention Metrics First** — Cohort retention > vanity growth. 5% churn reduction = 25-95% profit increase.
9. **Sales Playbook by Deal #10** — Document what works before hiring reps. Founder-led sales must become repeatable.
10. **Test Pricing Quarterly** — Most companies leave 15-25% revenue on the table. Price on value delivered, not cost.
11. **Email: Segment + Personalize + Test** — Segmented campaigns drive 760% more revenue than batch sends (DMA). A/B test subject lines, send times, CTAs per segment.
12. **B2B: Multi-Thread the Account** — Engage 3+ stakeholders per deal. Single-threaded deals die when the champion leaves or gets overruled. Gong data: multi-threaded deals close 2x more.
13. **Content Pillars + Distribution Flywheel** — Build 3-5 pillar topics aligned to ICP pain points; repurpose each piece across channels (blog->social->email->webinar).
14. **Define ICP With 5+ Attributes** — Firmographic + behavioral criteria. Say no to off-profile prospects. Without ICP, marketing is scattered and sales cycles are 3x longer.
15. **Win/Loss Analysis After Every Significant Deal** — Interview customers who chose you AND those who chose competitors. Reveals real positioning and objections sales missed.
